Guacamole

Guacamole is a traditional dip in mexican culture

Guacamole is an internationally recognized dish with history dating back to the pre-colombian era of mexico. This dish is best prepared fresh using a traditional mexican mortar and pestle made of lava rock, called a "molcajete"

Ingredients
  • avocados
  • onion
  • garlic
  • salt
  • tomatoes
  • limes
  • cilantro
Steps
  1. Dice the tomatoes, onions and finely chop the cilantro
  2. Grind the salt and garlic into a fine paste
  3. Add in the avocados and mash them
  4. Add in the tomatoes, onions, and cilantro to the mixture
  5. Mix in the lime juice to taste
